The Importance of Seek Knowledge

In Islam, seeking knowledge is a fundamental duty for every individual. It is through understanding and reflection that we strengthen our faith and contribute positively to the world around us.

وَقُل رَّبِّ زِدْنِي عِلْمًا "And say: My Lord, increase me in knowledge."
— Surah Taha (20:114)

The Five Pillars of Islam

These are the core beliefs and practices that shape the life of a Muslim:

  • Shahada: Profession of Faith
  • Salah: Daily Prayers
  • Zakat: Almsgiving to the needy
  • Sawm: Fasting during the month of Ramadan
  • Hajj: Pilgrimage to Makkah

Daily Mindfulness

Remembering Allah throughout the day (Dhikr) provides peace to the heart. Simple phrases like SubhanAllah (Glory be to Allah), Alhamdulillah (Praise be to Allah), and Allahu Akbar (Allah is Greatest) can transform our daily routine into an act of worship.
